About Me

Hi there!

I'm Loïc, a Software Dev in training!
I've always been drawn to creative and innovative industries, especially the exciting worlds of video games, automotive, and aerospace. Whether it’s racing teams or cutting-edge car and plane manufacturers, working on cool projects that push boundaries is what excites me most.

But my journey to software engineering wasn’t exactly linear. I first dived into an Economy and Management Master’s program, only to realize that selling things just wasn’t my thing. So, I took a gap year in the UK, and ended up working for an awesome company specializing in video game Localization and Quality Assurance. I had an amazing time testing games, fixing bugs, and collaborating with developers to tackle challenges creatively. It was such a blast!

When I returned to France, I stumbled upon a unique opportunity—a coding school called 42 opening up near my hometown in Perpignan. It’s a one-of-a-kind place where there are no teachers or traditional classes; instead, you learn by working on real projects alongside peers. That’s where I discovered the fascinating world of software engineering, and honestly, I’ve been hooked ever since. Now, I’m all about combining creativity and technical skills to build amazing things. Whether it’s developing software, diving into game development, or exploring the world of web development, I’m ready to tackle exciting challenges and keep learning every step of the way.
